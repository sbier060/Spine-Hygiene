SpineIQ

A posture coach that lives in your menu bar — and never phones home.

Spine-IQ watches how you sit through your Mac's camera, entirely on your Mac. When you slouch, it tells you — out loud, by name. Everything else it just quietly tracks: sitting vs. standing, desk transitions, percent good posture per day, per spot in your home.

Install (macOS) curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/sbier060/Spine-Hygiene/main/scripts/install.sh | sh

One command, no account, no App Store. The app keeps itself up to date automatically.

  • Speaks up when you slouch — "Alek, sit up. You want to be able to pick up Jack when you're older."
  • Knows sitting from standing — it detects your adjustable desk physically moving.
  • Learns your spots — desk, comfy chair, couch — each judged by its own baseline.
  • Camera off when you're away — the green light honestly tracks you being at the desk.
  • Radically private — no cloud, no account, no frame ever stored or uploaded. Read the privacy doc.
Open source on GitHub. Spine-IQ is a wellness utility, not a medical device.
